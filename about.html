Visit your Dashboard to see your streak, completion rate, and progress visualizations.

Pro Tips for Success

💡 Tip #1: Consistency Over Perfection

Building habits is about consistency, not perfection. Even on difficult days, do something is better than nothing.

💡 Tip #2: Same Time, Every Day

Complete your habit at the same time each day. This helps create a routine and makes it easier to remember.

💡 Tip #3: Track Immediately

Log your completion right after you finish. This reinforces the habit and keeps your records accurate.

💡 Tip #4: Celebrate Small Wins

Celebrate every completed day. These small wins accumulate into major life transformations.

💡 Tip #5: Use the 21-Day Rule

Research shows it takes about 21 days to form a habit. Push through the first three weeks!

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I miss a day?

If you miss a day, your streak resets, but you can start a new one immediately. The key is getting back on track!

Can I customize my habits?

Currently, we offer curated daily challenges, but you can use Strict Mode to define your own goals.

How often are new habits added?

Our habit database cycles every 30 days, giving you a fresh rotation of challenges.

Is my data private?

Yes! Your habit data is private and secure. We never share your information with third parties.

How do I reset my progress?

You can reset your progress from your account settings. This gives you a fresh start whenever you need it.
